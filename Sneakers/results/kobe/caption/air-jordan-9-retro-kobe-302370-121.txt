Air Jordan 9 Retro 'Kobe' | SKU: 302370 121 | Released: 2016-11-19


The Air Jordan 9 Retro ‘Kobe’ is inspired by the player exclusive model worn by Kobe Bryant during his period of sneaker free agency in the 2002-03 season. Released in November of 2016, the design features a white leather upper with concord patent leather overlays and tour yellow accents. The sneaker also replaces the original’s #8 with the Jordan Brand’s signature #23 on its heels.
