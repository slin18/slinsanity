Crazy 1 'Metallic Silver' 2010 | SKU: 467312 | Released: nan


Initially dubbed 'The Kobe,' the adidas Crazy 1 was first released in 2000 on the feet of the legendary hoops star. Inspired by the Audi TT Roadster, this 'Metallic Silver' iteration is constructed with a synthetic upper and highlighted by black finishes to create a futuristic look. Complete with a double foam collar at the ankle, a durable half-shell construction at the toe and an injected EVA midsole down below, the basketball shoe supports multi-directional movement on and off the court.
