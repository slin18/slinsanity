Hyperdunk '08 'Fade To Black' | SKU: 869611 001 | Released: 2016-03-28


The Nike Hyperdunk ’08 ‘FTB’ released in March 2016 as part of the larger Fade to Black pack, which celebrated the final season of Kobe Bryant’s NBA career through a collection of his signature Nike sneakers. The Hyperdunk ’08 is finished in a blend of Stealth and Cool Grey, with custom sockliner graphics that give the nod to Bryant’s gold medal victory at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.
