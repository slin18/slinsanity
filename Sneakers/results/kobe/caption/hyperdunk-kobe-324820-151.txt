Hyperdunk 'Kobe' | SKU: 324820 151 | Released: nan


Nike created the Hyperdunk ‘Kobe’ at the end of the basketball icon's 2007-08 NBA Season, leading into the 2008 Olympic Games. Bryant donned the sneakers to represent team USA on the court in Beijing. The shoe features Los Angeles Lakers team colors as a nod to Bryant's team. Innovations including Flywire cables and Lunar Foam provide an locked-in fit and soft, cushioned feel.
