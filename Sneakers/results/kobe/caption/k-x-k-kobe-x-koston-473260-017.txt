SB 'Kobe x Koston' | SKU: 473260 017 | Released: 2011-05-21


Limited to just 24 pairs, this Nike SB 'Kobe x Koston’ was the first official collaboration between basketball star Kobe Bryant and skateboarder Eric Koston. The shoe combines the snakeskin-like upper of the Kobe 6 with Koston’s revolutionary Lunarlon cushioned lo ride cupsole tooling. Each pair came packaged with a custom humidor, signature Nike Eric Koston golf balls, and a suede shoe bag.
