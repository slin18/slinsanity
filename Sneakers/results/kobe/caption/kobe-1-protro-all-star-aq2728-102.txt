Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'All-Star' | SKU: AQ2728 102 | Released: 2018-08-17


Drawing from the sneaker Kobe Bryant wore during the 2006 NBA All-Star Game, this colorway is the Zoom Kobe 1’s 2018 comeback. The Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'All-Star' features a white leather upper with black accents on the Swoosh, carbon fiber, toe and leather band that spans from the medial side to the heel. The basketball sneaker also boasts debossed ‘All-Star 2006’ lettering on the tongue and Varsity Red details on the midsole and collar.
