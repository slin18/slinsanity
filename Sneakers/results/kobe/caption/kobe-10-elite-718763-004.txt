Kobe 10 Elite 'Commander' | SKU: 718763 004 | Released: 2015-10-14


Kobe Bryant made a tribute to then-President Barack Obama in October 2015 with this special Kobe 10 Elite clad in team colors from the Commander in Chief’s Honolulu high school. Based around an all-black Flyknit upper, the silhouette features bright Blue Lagoon and Volt accents, including a matching blue translucent outsole. A Latin expression translating to “history is life’s teacher” adorns the tongue alongside a vibrant Volt eagle.
