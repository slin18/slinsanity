Kobe 10 Elite 'Fade To Black | SKU: 869458 441 | Released: 2016-04-11


In the lead up to Kobe Bryant’s retirement, Nike released a Fade to Black collection that included new colorways for all of Kobe Bryant’s signature models. This Nike Kobe 10 Elite Low ‘FTB’ was the penultimate release in the collection. It features a Dark Obsidian Flyknit upper sporting dynamic Flywire technology, with tonal Swooshes blended into the design. The leather tongue has embossed Kobe branding, while Lunarlon and Zoom Air provide cushioning. Color arrives via the gold accents on the insole.
