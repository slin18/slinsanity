Kobe 10 EP 'Pain' | SKU: 745334 001 | Released: 2015-07-09


Released in 2015, the Nike Kobe 10 ‘Pain’ pays tribute to Kobe Bryant’s incredible tolerance as proven by a memorable performance in which he lead his team to a state championship with a broken nose. The design’s mesh upper is covered in 3M reflective material and a heat-activated print that shows through the shoe’s clear rubber outsole.
