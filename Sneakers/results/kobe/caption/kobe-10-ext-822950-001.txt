Kobe 10 High EXT 'Black' | SKU: 822950 001 | Released: 2015-12-11


Nike’s tenth signature shoe for Kobe Bryant is modeled after armor, with a ready-for-battle aesthetic. The textured upper features an embossed pattern of Bryant’s sheath logo and an extended high-top collar that climbs up the ankle. The Kobe 10 offers enhanced traction with its unique outsole pattern, with further support provided by a carbon-fiber shank. The black design up top is accented by hits of metallic gold, while a gum outsole complements the look.
