Kobe 10 GS 'Blackout' | SKU: 726067 005 | Released: 2015-02-21


Like the men’s version, the Kobe 10 GS ‘Blackout’ is inspired by Kobe Bryant’s rigorous workout routine. Persian Violet coloring, inspired by the only team Kobe played for, finishes both the seamless textile upper and translucent rubber midsole. The latter houses a hybrid cushioning system made up of full-length Lunarlon foam and a Zoom Air unit in the heel. Underfoot, a uniquely textured outsole delivers enhanced traction on the court.
