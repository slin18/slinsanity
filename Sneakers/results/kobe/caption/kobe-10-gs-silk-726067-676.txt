Kobe 10 GS 'Silk' | SKU: 726067 676 | Released: nan


The Kobe 10 GS 'Silk' gives Kobe Bryant's tenth signature shoe with Nike a colorway of Merlot, Metallic Gold, Villain Red and orange. A silhouette based on Bryant's dedicated approach to the game and his legendary will to win, this shoe draws inspiration from his international upbringing and Silk Road, a 1st-century trade route between Europe and Asia. Merlot is found on the shoe's tongue, laces and heel counter, while orange takes over most of the translucent textile upper and Nike Swoosh. Finishing the style is a Lunarlon cushioned midsole and translucent orange outsole.
