Kobe 10 '2014 Drew League Champion' iD | SKU: 777411 991 | Released: 2015-05-18


The Nike Kobe 10 iD 2014 ‘Drew League’ was made to celebrate the 2014 Drew League Champions, Hank’s Houdini’s All-Stars. The design features an Engineered Mesh upper in black, accented with metallic gold on the Swoosh, tongue and heel. ‘Drew’ and ‘Champs’ are embroidered inside the tongues of the left and right shoes, while the translucent midsole is sprinkled with gold speckled detailing.
