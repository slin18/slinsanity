The Nike Kobe 10 Elite unites three advanced technologies in a sophisticated, mid-top profile representative of Bryant's alter ego, Black Mamba. Launched in June 2015, this 'Rose Gold' variant supplies full-length support through an engineered mesh bootie and Flywire cables integrated into the laces.
