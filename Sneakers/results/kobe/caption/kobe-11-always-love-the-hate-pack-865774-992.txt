Kobe 11 'Always Love The Hate' Pack | SKU: 865774 992 | Released: 2016-04-13


Kobe Bryant’s Always Love the Hate pack celebrates his 20-year career in the NBA, featuring two special renditions of his signature Kobe 11. The first sneaker is the last shoe that released in his Fade to Black collection—and the one he wore in his final game—featuring an all-black upper with gold contrasting on the Swoosh. The second offering was made available on NIKEiD at the start of Bryant’s final game. Displaying a black Engineered Mesh upper and translucent blue Zoom Air outsole, the sneaker is contrasted with gold branding as well as various numbers that are relevant to the Bryant’s career.
