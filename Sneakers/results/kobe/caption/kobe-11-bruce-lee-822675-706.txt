Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Bruce Lee' | SKU: 822675 706 | Released: 2016-03-19


Released in March 2016, the Nike Kobe 11 'Bruce Lee' features a yellow and black Flyknit upper inspired by the jumpsuit worn by Bruce Lee in his final film 'Game of Death.' The shoe also comes with an all-black sole and red scratch marks on the heel as a nod to Lee's wounds in the final fight in 'Enter the Dragon.' The Bruce Lee theme was also featured on the Zoom Kobe 5 in 2010.
