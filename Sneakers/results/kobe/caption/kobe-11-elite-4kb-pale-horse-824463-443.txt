The Nike Kobe 11 Elite 4KB distills Bryant's legacy into a sophisticated, high-performance design. Emerged in October 2016, this 'Pale Horse' rendition celebrates the Day of the Dead with skeletal ribbing on the Flyknit upper's heel and a skull insole graphic. Flywire cables are reintegrated into the midfoot. Benefiting from Lunarlon and Zoom Air tech, the drop-in midsole and translucent outsole optimize impact defense and traction.
