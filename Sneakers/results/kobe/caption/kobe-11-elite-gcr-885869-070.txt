Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Great Career Recall' | SKU: 885869 070 | Released: 2016-06-25


Following his 2016 retirement, Nike saluted Kobe Bryant’s legendary NBA career with the ‘GCR’ edition of the Kobe 11 Elite, as in ‘Great Career Recall.’ The black and gold low-cut sneaker features a Flyknit upper with Lunarlon foam and Zoom Air cushioning in the sole. The commemorative colorway comes with a custom gold wreath and dual star emblem on the heel tab, while the red Kobe four-stripe branding is positioned just below.
