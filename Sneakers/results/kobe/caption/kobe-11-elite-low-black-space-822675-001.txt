Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Black Space' | SKU: 822675 001 | Released: 2016-10-04


The Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Black Space' features a black Flyknit upper with a reflective black Swoosh, black suede tongue, and an ice-blue translucent sole. The shoe also comes with black suede heel panels with Kobe's 'Achilles' logo in bright red on the left shoe, and an 'XX' embroidered onto the right. Released in October 2016, the colorway was part of Nike's 'Black Space' pack that also included blacked out colorways for the KD 9 and LeBron Soldier 10 silhouettes.
