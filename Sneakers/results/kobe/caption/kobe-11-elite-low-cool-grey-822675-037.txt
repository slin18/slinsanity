Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Master of Innovation' | SKU: 822675 037 | Released: 2016-08-11


The ‘Master of Innovation’ offering of the Kobe 11 Elite Low features a Cool Grey Flyknit construction on the upper that fades to Yellow Strike and Voltage Green hues on the toe. Kobe’s signature red Achilles tendon design appears on the left heel, a nod to his return to the NBA after tearing the tendon, while the right shoe features a graphic embroidered with ‘96-03’ on the right heel. The Flyknit cables on the eyestay are included for a locked-down fit.
