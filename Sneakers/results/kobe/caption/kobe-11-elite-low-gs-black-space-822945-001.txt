Kobe 11 Elite Low GS 'Black Space' | SKU: 822945 001 | Released: nan


Made for big kids, the Nike Kobe 11 Elite Low GS ‘Black Space’ features a subdued take on the performance low-top. A black reflective Swoosh adorns the matching black knit upper, stitched directly onto the transparent rubber outsole. Contrasting pops of red stand out on the stitch marks embroidered on the left heel, a nod to Kobe’s Achilles injury. Lightweight cushioning comes courtesy of a drop-in Lunarlon midsole with Zoom Air cushioning in the heel.
