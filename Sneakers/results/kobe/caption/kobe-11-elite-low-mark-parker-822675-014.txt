Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Mark Parker' | SKU: 822675 014 | Released: 2016-05-26


The final signature sneaker of Kobe Bryant's playing career, the Kobe 11 debuted in 2015. Part of Nike’s 2016 Kobe 11 Muse Pack, this special edition Kobe 11 Elite Low ‘Mark Parker’ was designed by the Nike CEO. It features a black and blue remixed Flyknit pattern and an embossed textured snakeskin panel on the heel. Cushioning is provided by Lunarlon and Nike Zoom Air technologies, while the outsole reveals a translucent application underfoot.
