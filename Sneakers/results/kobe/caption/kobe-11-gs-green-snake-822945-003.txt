Kobe 11 GS 'Green Snake' | SKU: 822945 003 | Released: 2016-08-08


Kobe Bryant’s 11th sneaker with Nike appears as a kids’ exclusive with the ‘Green Snake.’ The shoe gets its name from the green and black pattern that emerges on the seamless mesh upper, which is contrasted with a silver Nike logo and neon yellow Kobe branding.
