Kobe 11 'Lower Merion' | SKU: 836183 006 | Released: 2016-04-09


The Nike Kobe 11 ‘Lower Merion’ is a tribute Kobe Bryant’s alma mater, Philadelphia’s Lower Merion High School. Dressed in the school’s colors, the shoe’s Engineered Mesh upper combines Cool Grey, Wolf Grey and Team Red with 3M reflective highlights on the mudguard and eyestay. The low-top shoe includes a Lower Merion Aces logo on the right heel, with Bryant’s signature stitches on the left.
