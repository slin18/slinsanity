Kobe 11 Elite Low 'Mambacurial Barcelona' | SKU: 844130 464 | Released: 2016-09-08


A tribute to Kobe Bryant’s love of soccer, this Kobe 11 Mambacurial ‘Barcelona' comes dressed in the colors of his favorite club team, FC Barcelona. The low top shoe features a premium Flyknit upper with a College Navy base and a gradient fade to University Red and Racer Blue on the lateral and medial sides. Soccer badge-inspired heel branding compliments the orange Swoosh branding – nodding to FCB’s away uniform – and the look is finished off with a classic gum rubber outsole underfoot.
