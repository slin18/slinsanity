Kobe 11 'EYBL Peach Jam' | SKU: 856852 282 | Released: 2017-07-01


The Kobe 11 'EYBL Peach Jam’ colorway was created for 2016’s Nike Elite Youth Basketball League Tournament, a.k.a. the Peach Jam. It features a Pearl White engineered mesh upper with Atomic Orange accents and a lime green lining. It also comes with an icy blue translucent outsole with the EYBL logo on the right foot and PEACH JAM branding on the left foot. The low top sneaker also comes with a Zoom Air unit in the heel and a drop-in Lunarlon foam sockliner for lightweight cushioning.
