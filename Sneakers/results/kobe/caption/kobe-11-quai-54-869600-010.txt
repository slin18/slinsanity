Kobe 11 'Quai 54' | SKU: 869600 010 | Released: 2016-07-09


Quai 54 is an annual basketball tournament that takes place in the heart of Paris. To celebrate the 2016 iteration, Nike released the Quai 54 collection. The Kobe 11 'Quai 54' features an engineered mesh upper dressed in white with black underlays and Flywire cables on the lacing system for support. The low top also sports black leather details on the collar and ankle lining, embroidered Quai 54 branding at heel, and a custom graphic on the outsole.
