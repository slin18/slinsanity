In 2013, Nike released the ‘Kobe Prelude Pack,’ an eight-part collection with each shoe embodying a different style of art. This Kobe 2 GS was inspired by Kobe’s milestone of playing four games where he scored over 50 points and features a marbled texture and gold accents as a tribute to his achievement.
