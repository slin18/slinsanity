Inspired by the Los Angeles Lakers’ defeat to the Boston Celtics in the 2008 NBA Finals, Nike released a colorway to attest to Kobe Bryant’s resilience. The Kobe 3 GS 'Prelude' boasts a synthetic waffled upper with a flaming galactic print throughout, as well as leather accents on the heel and toe box. The sneaker also features purple detailing on the midsole liner and eyelets, with multicolor Swoosh branding on the heel.
