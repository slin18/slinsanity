Zoom Kobe 4 'MLK' | SKU: 344335 171 | Released: 2009-05-08


Kobe Bryant wore the Nike Kobe 4 ‘MLK’ during a Los Angeles Lakers game held on Martin Luther King Jr. Day in 2009. Bryant’s first low-top silhouette, the shoe features a white synthetic base with supporting Flywire cables and a gradient Varsity Purple finish on the toe. Contrasting pops of metallic gold appear on the Swoosh and collar, with the latter treated to a subtle snakeskin texture.
