Zoom Kobe 5 GS 'Lakers' | SKU: 386647 071 | Released: 2010-01-08


The Nike Zoom Kobe 5 GS 'Lakers' pays tribute to the only professional team Kobe Bryant played for during his 20-year NBA career. Dressed in black, yellow and Varsity Purple, this shoe is sized specifically for kids. Released in January 2010, its low-top silhouette includes the Kobe 'Sheath' logo and Nike Swoosh in yellow and perforations on its black toe box. Created to be a lighter sneaker without compromising stability, it also features Nike's Flywire technology and Nike Zoom units in the heel and forefoot.
