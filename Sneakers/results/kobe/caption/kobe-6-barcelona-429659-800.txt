The Zoom Kobe 6 ‘Barcelona’ takes inspiration from Bryant’s father’s European legacy as a professional basketball player. In FCB team red, the design features a snakeskin-textured upper contrasted by a black Swoosh and robust TPU heel counter. The contrast continues underfoot with a black midsole topped off by a translucent rubber outsole.
