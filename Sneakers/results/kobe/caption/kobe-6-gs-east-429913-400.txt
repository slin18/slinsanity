Zoom Kobe 6 GS 'East LA' | SKU: 429913 400 | Released: nan


Released in January 2011, the Nike Zoom Kobe 6 GS 'East LA' comes decorated in a Drenched Blue, Metallic Silver and Sport Red colorway. This kids' shoe features 'The Sheath' logo on the tongue and the Zoom Kobe 6's snakeskin design—a nod to Kobe's 'Black Mamba' nickname—with hints of the East LA skyline on the upper. Bryant's signature resides on the heel with a translucent outsole completing the model.
