Zoom Kobe 7 'All Star - Galaxy' | SKU: 520810 001 | Released: 2012-02-24


This Nike Zoom Kobe 7 All Star 'Galaxy' colorway features a Supernova print on the upper and inner lining with a metallic silver Swoosh. It also comes with a NASA-inspired Black Mamba mission patch on the heel, a black midsole, and a glow-in-the-dark outsole. It dropped for the 2012 All-Star Game in Orlando alongside ‘Galaxy’ themed Zoom KD 4s, LeBron 9s, and Air Foamposite 1s.
