Kobe 8 GS 'Extraterrestrial' | SKU: 555586 800 | Released: nan


Designed for the 2013 NBA All-Star Game held in Houston, the Kobe 8 GS ‘Extraterrestrial’ features inspiration from the city’s famous mission control center. The shoe’s Engineered Mesh upper is stamped with swirling orange hues that suggest an extraterrestrial planet, while a white Zoom midsole provides cushioning. Contrasting color arrives via the Sport Turquoise Swoosh.
