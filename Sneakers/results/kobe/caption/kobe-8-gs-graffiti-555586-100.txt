Kobe 8 GS 'Graffiti' | SKU: 555586 100 | Released: nan


The eighth signature sneaker for Kobe Bryant, the Kobe 8 GS 'Graffiti' applies a unique color scheme to its low-cut silhouette. Released in 2013 and dressed in white, black, Dark Grey and Flash Lime, this shoe is sized specifically for kids. Black and shades of grey dominate the graffiti-themed upper, while lime provides pop on the lining, Swoosh and Bryant branding. The lightweight shoe gets its cushioning from Nike's Lunarlon midsole, while a grey outsole promotes superior traction.
