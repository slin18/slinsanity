Kobe 8 System 'Blitz Blue' | SKU: 555035 401 | Released: 2013-06-29


The release of the Kobe 8 System was a game changer, offering the lowest and lightest performance basketball shoe at just 9.6 ounces. Continuing to expand its legacy, Nike released the Kobe 8 System 'Blitz Blue.' The low top boasts a Blitz Blue engineered mesh upper with a reinforced navy toe box and heel for structure and durability. Other design details include a padded collar for comfort, and Total Crimson accents on the laces, Swoosh, and on the Kobe signature at the heel.
