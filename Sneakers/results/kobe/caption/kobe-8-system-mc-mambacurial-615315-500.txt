Kobe 8 System 'Mambacurial' | SKU: 615315 500 | Released: 2013-06-07


Inspired by Kobe Bryant’s passion for soccer, the Kobe 8 System Mc ‘Mambacurial’ takes its design cues from the Nike Mercurial Vapor 9 soccer boot. The design features a gradient fade on the upper from Red Plum at the heel to Pink Flash at the toe, with Electric Green accents on the tongue, Swoosh and outsole. Cushioning arrives via Lunarlon and Zoom Air.
