The Nike Kobe 8 System ‘Playoff’ released in May 2013, during the NBA postseason. The performance basketball shoe is built with a tightly woven Engineered Mesh upper, adorned in a purple snakeskin print inspired by Kobe Bryant’s Black Mamba persona.
