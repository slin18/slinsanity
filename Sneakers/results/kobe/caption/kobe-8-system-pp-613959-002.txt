Kobe 8 System 'Philippines Pack - Gym Red' | SKU: 613959 002 | Released: 2013-09-07


The Nike Kobe 8 System 'Philippines' is one of two colorways created for the UAAP Basketball Tournament held in the Philippines. Released in 2013 and dressed in black, Metallic Silver, Gym Red and Bright Crimson, the shoe features a black Engineered Mesh upper with the Nike Swoosh in silver. Signature branding is found on the tongue and heel, both of which are coated in crimson. The full-length Lunarlon midsole and inner lining come with jagged graphics, while the rubber outsole includes a snake-scale-inspired traction pattern.
