Kobe 8 System 'Python' | SKU: 555035 300 | Released: 2013-02-06


The KOBE 8 System Elite dropped during the 2013 postseason. Designed by Eric Avar, the shoe features Kevlar-reinforced dynamic Flywire technology for a locked-down fit, a thicker engineered mesh upper than the regular season model, an articulated foam tongue for impact protection, and a carbon fiber heel clip for improved stability. This limited release ‘Python’ model celebrates the 2013 Chinese New Year and the ushering of the Year of the Snake. It features a Squadron Green upper with Challenge Red and Legion Brown accents.
