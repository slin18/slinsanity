Kobe 8 System 'Year of the Horse' | SKU: 639654 674 | Released: 2014-01-01


The Kobe 8 System ‘Year of the Horse’ dropped on New Year’s Day in 2014. Paying tribute to 2014 being the Year of the Horse, the low-top features a Light Crimson, Bright Citron and Vivid Blue colorway inspired by the colors most likely to bring luck in that year. It also features Kobe Bryant’s signature on the heel counter and a Lunarlon midsole for cushioning.
