Kobe 9 Elite GS 'Perspective' | SKU: 636602 400 | Released: 2014-03-08


Kobe Bryant merges personal storytelling with an innovative design on the ‘Perspective’ iteration of the Kobe 9 Elite. The colorway pays tribute to Pablo Picasso’s Blue Period, with a teal Flyknit construction on the upper that sports cubist designs on the ankle. The shoe is contrasted with Volt accents throughout, while the Flywire cables help create locked-down support.
