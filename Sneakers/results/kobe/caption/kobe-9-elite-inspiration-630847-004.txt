Kobe 9 Elite 'Inspiration' | SKU: 630847 004 | Released: 2014-03-01


The Kobe 9 Elite ‘Inspiration’ puts a simple colorway on the ultra-high silhouette, with an Anthracite upper contrasted by hits of yellow on the Flyknit upper.
