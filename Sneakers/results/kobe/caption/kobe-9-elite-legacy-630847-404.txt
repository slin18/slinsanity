Kobe 9 Elite 'Brave Blue' | SKU: 630847 404 | Released: 2014-10-18


The ‘Brave Blue’ edition of the Kobe 9 Elite features a Flyknit upper equipped with dynamic Flywire tooling and carbon fiber elements at the midfoot, heel and on portions of the sidewall. A Lunarlon sole adds cushioning underfoot, while the embroidered words ‘KB NINTH’ and stitches to represent Kobe’s injury bring an additional design touch to the elevated collar.
