Kobe 9 Elite Low 'Beethoven' | SKU: 639045 101 | Released: 2014-08-16


Inspired by the music composer from the 1800’s, the Nike Kobe 9 Elite “Beethoven” is meant to reflect the idea of timelessness with a simple white and black colorway. Constructed with a grey Flyknit upper, the silhouette is contrasted with white tones on the tongue and heel, while black hues line the branding and Swoosh. Additionally, a carbon fiber print is featured on the heel, while a speckled midsole rounds out the monochromatic appeal.
