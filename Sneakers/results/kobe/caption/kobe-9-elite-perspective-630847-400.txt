Kobe 9 Elite 'Perspective' | SKU: 630847 400 | Released: 2014-03-08


Inspired by Pablo Picasso and his ‘Blue Period’ the Kobe 9 Elite ‘Perspective’ features shades of blue with graphics on the upper that nod to the Spanish master’s art. One of the first colorways to emerge of the Nike Kobe 9 Elite, the silhouette is constructed with a seamless Flyknit fabric on the upper that sports a turquoise hue, while a Volt Swoosh provide a stark contrast.The shoe is set on a light blue midsole, while subtle red tones appear on the back of the sock-liner.
