Kobe 9 Premium HTM 'Milan - Wolf Grey' | SKU: 698595 010 | Released: 2014-04-08


The HTM series is an expansive, collaborative body of work from Hiroshi Fujiwara, Tinker Hatfield and Mark Parker that celebrates the possibilities offered by Flyknit technology. The Kobe 9 Premium 'HTM' features a Wolf Grey Flyknit upper with reflective flecked laces and Flywire cables. The reflective detailing also makes its way to the Swooshes, while an ultrathin web outsole rests underfoot.
