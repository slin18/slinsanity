Kobe A.D. Exodus 'Green Strike' | SKU: AR5515 301 | Released: 2018-09-24


Kobe Bryant and Nike have once again collaborated for the Kobe A.D. Exodus 'Green Strike' sneaker. Released in November 2018 as part of a seven-shoe pack, the Kobe A.D. features a light mesh upper with padded foam overlays and a motion-enhancing, low-cut collar.
