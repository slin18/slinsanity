Kobe A.D. 2018 'Red Orbit' | SKU: AV5515 601 | Released: nan


Released in late 2018, the Kobe A.D. 2018 'Red Orbit' was part of a 'Color Pack' that offered seven different team-inspired combinations. Red Orbit coats the majority of this low-top performance shoe with Clear Emerald accents, most notably on the quarter panel's Nike Swoosh. Highlights of the sneaker include Kobe Bryant signature detailing, pull tabs on the tongue and heel, and a Zoom Air unit to promote responsive cushioning.
