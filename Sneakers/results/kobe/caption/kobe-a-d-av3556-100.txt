Kobe A.D. Exodus EP 'Sail Multicolor' | SKU: AV3556 100 | Released: 2018-08-24


This addition to the Black Mamba’s signature Nike line released on August 24, 2018—aka Kobe Bryant Day, as established by the Los Angeles City Council in 2016. The Kobe A.D. EP ‘Sail’ sports a textile upper with a neutral grey base, accented with navy on the toe and pops of red and yellow on the speed-lacing system. A white midsole, housing a Zoom Air unit for lightweight cushioning, is supported underfoot by a bright yellow rubber outsole.
