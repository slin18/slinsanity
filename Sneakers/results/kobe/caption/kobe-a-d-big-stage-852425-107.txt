Kobe A.D. 'Big Stage' | SKU: 852425 107 | Released: 2017-06-03


In 2010, Kobe Bryant wore his fifth signature shoe to defeat to Boston Celtics. Seven years later, Nike released a commemorative exclusive in a similar color scheme. The Kobe A.D. 'Big Stage' features an engineered white mesh upper with Flywire technology on the side panels for added support. The low top also boasts metallic gold accents on the side Swoosh, a synthetic reinforcing heel counter, and a padded collar for comfort.
