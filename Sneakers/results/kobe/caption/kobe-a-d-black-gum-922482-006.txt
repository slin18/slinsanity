Kobe A.D. Mid 'Black Gum' | SKU: 922482 006 | Released: 2017-12-16


The Kobe A.D. was the first shoe Kobe Bryant released with Nike after his retirement. The Kobe A.D. Mid 'Black Gum' features a black suede upper with Flywire technology along the sides for added support. The sneaker also boasts black leather details on the heel for structure, a speckled white midsole with added cushioning, and a holographic pink Swoosh on the side panel with matching tongue branding.
