Kobe A.D. Mid 'Fearless' | SKU: 922482 500 | Released: 2017-08-24


The Nike Kobe A.D. Mid appears as part of a five-shoe pack that looks at the psychology of color, especially as it pertains to Kobe Bryant’s mentality on the court. The ‘Fearless’ offering appears in a tonal purple hue with a suede upper construction and speckled detailing on the midsole. According to Bryant, purple resonates with overcoming challenges and setting new goals while fearing virtually nothing.
