Kobe A.D. 2018 'Lakers Away' | SKU: AR5515 500 | Released: 2018-12-05


The Kobe A.D. ‘Lakers Pack’ pays homage to the Los Angeles team with two distinct colorways including this Hyper Grape iteration. The design is dipped in iconic L.A. Lakers purple with yellow accents over the medial sides and two-tone outsole. Other signature details include contrasting Kobe sheath logos at the tongue and heel, “KBB” on the inside tongue, and multicolor “KOBE” embroidery on the right heel. The design released alongside a contrasting yellow version in December 2018.
