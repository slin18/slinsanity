Kobe A.D. Mid 'NCAA Tournament' | SKU: AJ6921 001 | Released: 2018-03-22


Released to coincide with the 2018 NCAA Basketball Tournament, this edition of the Kobe A.D. features a black upper, speckled midsole, and an iridescent Swoosh. The standout element of the mid top silhouette is found on the outsole, where strokes of red, yellow, blue and purple dot a black base. The ‘NCAA Tournament’ colorway offers a unique take on the shoe that marked the end of the Black Mamba’s playing days.
