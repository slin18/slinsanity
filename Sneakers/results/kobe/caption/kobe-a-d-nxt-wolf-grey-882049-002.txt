Kobe A.D. NXT 'Wolf Grey' | SKU: 882049 002 | Released: 2017-04-01


The ‘Wolf Grey’ edition of the Kobe A.D. NXT features a bootie-style mesh construction overlaid with a Flyknit shroud. In a blend of style and function, the element not only conceals the laces but tightens them with a toggle positioned at the top of the tongue. Further design touches can be seen on the reversed midfoot Swoosh and contrasting Kobe branding on the heels, while the translucent outsole is equipped with Zoom Air cushioning.
