Kobe A.D. Mid 'Passion' | SKU: 922482 600 | Released: 2017-08-24


The ‘Passion’ Kobe AD Mid is one of five different tonal offerings in the 2017 ‘Mamba Mentality’ Pack that pays tribute to Kobe Bryant’s time playing for the L.A. Lakers. While other colors in the series stand for traits like calmness and positivity, the red rendition represents passion for the game. Unique to the red variation, a signature print on the heel, toe, and collar completes the limited edition look.
