Kobe A.D. TB 2018 'Midnight Navy' | SKU: AT3874 407 | Released: nan


This edition of the Nike Kobe A.D. is presented in a colorway of Midnight Navy and Metallic Silver. Tony Grosso designed the model which released in 2018 and features Kobe Bryant signature details throughout. His initials, KBB, appear on the inner tongue, his first name is displayed on the upper heel, and his Sheath logo resides near the ankle. The low-top sneaker comes with pull tabs on the tongue and heel, lace loops, a Lunarlon foam midsole and an embedded Zoom Air unit in the heel.
