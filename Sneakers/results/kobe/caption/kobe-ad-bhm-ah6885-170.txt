Kobe A.D. 'Black History Month' | SKU: AH6885 170 | Released: nan


For Black History Month 2017, Nike released a two-tone colorway that advocates for equality. The Kobe A.D. 'BHM' features a knit upper divided into two colors: white on the forefoot and midfoot, and black leather on the heel and ankle. The sneaker boasts a subtle pattern on the white section, metallic gold details on the Swoosh and tongue branding, and University Red details on the heel branding. The sneaker also boasts Lunarlon foam for added comfort and a Zoom Air unit in the heel for responsive cushioning.
