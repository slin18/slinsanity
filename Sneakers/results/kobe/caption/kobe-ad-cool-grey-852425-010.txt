Kobe A.D. 'Cool Grey' | SKU: 852425 010 | Released: 2016-11-22


The first shoe in the Kobe lineage to release after his retirement, the Nike Kobe A.D. is constructed with a tonal ‘Cool Grey’ on the mesh and neoprene upper, while an iridescent accent appears on the back of the midsole and heel. The Flywire lacing system provides a locked down fit, while the Swoosh, branding, and interior angle of the midsole are contrasted in white.
