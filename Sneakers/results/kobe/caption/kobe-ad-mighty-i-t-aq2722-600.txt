Kobe A.D. 'Mighty I.T.' PE | SKU: AQ2721 600 | Released: 2018-01-06


Nike released an exclusive colorway to celebrate the return of Cleveland Cavaliers point guard Isaiah Thomas following his hip injury. The Kobe A.D. 'Mighty I.T.' PE features a Cavaliers-inspired color scheme with a Gym Red upper and University Gold details on the mini jewel-like Swoosh on the sides and on the branded tongue. The sneaker also boasts trademark “I.T” initials on the heel counter, an icy iridescent outsole, and a graphic on the insoles.
