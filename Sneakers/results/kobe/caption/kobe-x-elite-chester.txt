Kobe 10 Elite 'Rivalry' | SKU: 747212 818 | Released: 2015-07-22


The ‘Chester’ edition of the Nike Kobe 10 is inspired by the orange jerseys of the high school rival team Bryant helped beat before leading his own to win a state title. Up top, a Flyknit upper is covered in dual orange tones and accentuated by a black toe box. The tribute continues underfoot with a white Zoom-cushioned midsole complemented by an orange translucent outsole.
