The ‘American’ offering of the Kobe 10 Elite features a USA color palette throughout the makeup of the performance basketball shoe. Red and navy accents are woven into the form-fitting design of the Flyknit upper, while the tongue logo is reworked into a stamp seal logo with a patriotic theme.
