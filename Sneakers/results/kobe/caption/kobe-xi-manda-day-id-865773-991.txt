Kobe 11 'Mamba Day' iD | SKU: 865773 99X | Released: 2016-04-13


The Kobe 11 'Mamba Day' iD was a limited release shoe that dropped during Kobe Bryant's final NBA game against the Utah Jazz on April 13th, 2016. The special edition NIKEiD shoe features a black Flyknit upper with a metallic gold Swoosh and a translucent ice-blue sole. The upper also features metallic gold graphics that highlight eight of Bryant's most impressive career stats. These include the dates of his first and last games (11.01.96 and 4.13.16), his five World Championships, his four All-Star MVP awards, his 18 All-Star appearances, his 81 points scored in a game, his 20 years in the NBA, and his jersey number '24.' Fans were also able to add a personal message on the inner tongue.
