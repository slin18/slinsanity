Mamba Instinct 'Anthracite' | SKU: 852473 006 | Released: 2018-05-05


Part of an inaugural quartet of colorways to introduce the Nike Mamba Instinct, the Nike Mamba Instinct 'Anthracite' sneaker is an OG model that dropped in January 2017. With a mix of Anthracite and Black hues, this low-top sneaker sports a seamless Flyweave upper with a graphic jacquard print woven throughout. The shoe features a silver Swoosh on the sidewall, a Black Mamba logo on the tongue and is finished with a full-length Lunarlon sole unit.
