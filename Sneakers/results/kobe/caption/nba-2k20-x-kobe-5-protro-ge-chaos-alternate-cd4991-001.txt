NBA 2K20 x Kobe 5 Protro 'Chaos Alternate' Gamer Exclusive | SKU: CD4991 001 | Released: 2019-12-26


The NBA 2K20 x Kobe 5 Protro ‘Chaos Alternate’ was available for purchase to players who successfully completed an in-game challenge on NBA 2K20. The sneaker itself is a revised version of the ‘Chaos’ colorway of the Kobe 5, featuring upgraded cushioning and dark grey paneling instead of the white finish of the original. The rest of the design remains mostly unchanged, highlighted by Volt accents and iridescent purple at the toe box and quarter panel.
