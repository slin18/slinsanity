Kobe 9 Elite Low 'Mamba Moment' iD | SKU: 677992 998 | Released: 2014-12-01


To commemorate Kobe Bryant becoming the NBA’s third-highest scorer of all time, Nike created a special iD option of the Kobe 9 Elite Low known as the ‘Mamba Moment.’ The shoe’s purple and green Flyknit upper was a pre-set design option, while the look is accented by gold on the tongue and heel branding as well as the Swoosh. A custom tongue tag sports Bryant’s initials as well as the point total that moved him into third place.
