Kobe 10 EP 'Fundamentals' | SKU: 745334 100 | Released: 2015-05-21


Adopting a ‘less is more’ mentality, the Kobe 10 emerges with a low-top construction that incorporates a hybrid cushioning unit in the midsole, featuring Lunarlon and Zoom Air. The outsole features siping grooves inspired by Nike Free to improve traction. The shoe’s open-weave textile on the seamless mesh upper was inspired by the texture of a shark’s skin, while the tongue incorporates a snakeskin detailing inspired by a mamba.
