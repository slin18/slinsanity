Kobe 10 'Easter' | SKU: 705317 808 | Released: 2015-04-02


The Nike Kobe 10 ‘Easter’ celebrates its namesake holiday with a color scheme that balances dark and light hues. The textile mesh upper is finished in Hot Lava, with complementary hits of black on the Swoosh and heel tab. The shoe’s laces, tongue and translucent midsole, which is equipped with a carbon fiber shank, are all done up in a vivid pastel shade that Nike calls Sunset Glow.
