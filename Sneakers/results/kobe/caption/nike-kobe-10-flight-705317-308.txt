Kobe 10 'Flight' | SKU: 705317 308 | Released: 2015-05-01


The Kobe 10 ‘Flight’ was released as part of a larger Flight collection that included the LeBron 12 and KD7. This colorway features a full, lightweight, breathable black mesh upper with hints of blue dispersed throughout the shoe.
