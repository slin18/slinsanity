Kobe 11 Elite Low 4KB 'Red Horse' | SKU: 824463 606 | Released: 2016-06-01


The Kobe 11 Elite Low 4KB 'Red Horse' provides detail and premium touches to Kevin Durant's 11th signature shoe, designed by Leo Chang. Its knit upper starts with a multicolor forefoot that gradually shifts into vertical tonal stripes, leading to a white heel guard accented by thick red stitching and a heel tab with varied icons. The shoe is complete with a mamba icon on the insole and a semi-translucent window on the outsole.
