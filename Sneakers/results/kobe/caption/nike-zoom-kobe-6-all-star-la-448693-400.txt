Zoom Kobe 6 'All Star - East LA' | SKU: 448693 400 | Released: 2011-02-16


The Zoom Kobe 6 ‘LA’ is the first of four colorways revealed in the ‘All Star’ pack commemorating the 2011 NBA All-Star Game. The special edition’s purple snakeskin upper depicts a graphic of the Los Angeles skyline along with tattoo-style artwork on the heel and insole. Other signature features include Kobe’s sheath logo in red, Kobe’s signature at the heel, and a translucent outsole.
