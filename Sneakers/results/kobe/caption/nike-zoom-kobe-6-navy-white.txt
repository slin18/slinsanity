Zoom Kobe 6 TB 'Midnight Navy' | SKU: 454142 401 | Released: 2011-07-01


The Zoom Kobe 6 TB is a part of Nike's Team Bank collection that comes in Midnight Navy, white and Metallic Silver. Released in 2011 and designed by Eric Avar, a reference to Kobe's 'Black Mamba' nickname is present in the form of a snakeskin effect on the upper. On the blue insole is 'KB' and 'I WANT A SHOE' on the left foot with '24' and 'THAT MOLDS TO MY FOOT' on the right foot. White coats the Nike Swoosh, the midsole and the outsole with its Nike Zoom units in the heel and forefoot.
