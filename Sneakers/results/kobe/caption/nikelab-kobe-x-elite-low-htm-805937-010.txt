Hiroshi Fujiwara, Tinker Hatfield, and Mark Parker combine as HTM on the NikeLab Kobe X Elite Low HTM. The cushioning system combines a Zoom Air midsole with Lunarlon technology. The Kobe X Elite takes inspiration from a variety of objects, including a shark jaw, a F-1 race car, and a mamba arrowhead among others.
