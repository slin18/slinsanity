Undefeated x Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'Green Camo' | SKU: BV1207 903 | Released: 2018-08-24


Los Angeles based sneaker boutique Undefeated teamed up with Nike to reissue special collabs of the Zoom Kobe 1 Protro. The four Player Exclusive offerings were designed as a tribute to the locations of Undefeated’s stores. This green camo iteration alludes to Undefeated’s Silver Lake location, featuring premium textiles and leather accents with the signature five-strike logo appearing on the heel.
