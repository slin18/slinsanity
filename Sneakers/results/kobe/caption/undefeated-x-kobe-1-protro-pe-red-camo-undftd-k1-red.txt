Undefeated x Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'Red Camo' | SKU: BV1207 900 | Released: 2018-08-24


Undefeated partnered with Nike on the Zoom Kobe 1 Protro for a Player Exclusive rendition of Kobe’s first signature silhouette with the sneaker brand. The shoes originally released in four different colorways of the camo textile to represent each one of UNDFTD’s US chapter stores. This red camo iteration was only available at the Santa Monica storefront and implements a carbon fiber accent on the midsole shank, with the brand’s classic five-strike logo on the heel.
