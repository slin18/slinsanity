Undefeated x Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'Yellow Camo' | SKU: BV1207 901 | Released: 2018-08-24


To celebrate Kobe Bryant Day in 2018—also known as August 24th—Undefeated teamed up with Nike to release the ‘Yellow Camo’ PE colorway of Zoom Kobe 1 Protro. The performance hoops shoe features a textile upper accented with a subtle yellow camo print and contrasting hits of black on the leather Swoosh and synthetic collar. The launch was limited to Undefeated’s La Brea flagship store in Los Angeles.
