Undefeated x Kobe 4 Protro 'Hyper Jade' | SKU: CQ3869 300 | Released: 2019-08-24


The Undefeated x Kobe 4 Protro 'DeMar DeRozan' was released on August 2019's Mamba Day. DeRozan debuted the shoe during the 2019 NBA Playoffs as his San Antonio Spurs took on the Denver Nuggets. Teal covers the sneaker's patent leather upper, while orange accents are found on the lining, tongue branding and around the quarter panel's black Nike Swoosh. Kobe's sheath logo appears on one shoe and the Undefeated five-strike logo is on the other, while 8 and 24 are each displayed on the heel to complete the style.
