Undefeated x Kobe 4 Protro 'Court Purple' | SKU: CQ3869 500 | Released: 2019-08-24


The Undefeated x Kobe 4 Protro 'Kyle Kuzma' comes in a colorway inspired by the Los Angeles Lakers, the team that drafted Kuzma in 2017 and where Kobe Bryant spent his entire 20-year NBA career. The shoe is one of four colorways released in August 2019 and features Kobe signature details, Undefeated branding and a purple patent leather upper. Both of Kobe's jersey numbers can be seen on the heel, along with a subtle 'UNDEFEATED' graphic underneath the heel counter.
