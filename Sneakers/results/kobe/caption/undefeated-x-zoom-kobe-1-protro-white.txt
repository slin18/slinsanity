Undefeated x Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'White Gum' | SKU: AQ3635 100 | Released: 2018-02-15


Kobe Bryant partnered up with LA-based streetwear company UNDEFEATED to release an exclusive colorway for NBA's 2018 All-Star Weekend. The Undefeated x Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'White Gum' features a white tumbled leather upper with black accents on the large Swoosh branding, ankle collar, and signature UNDEFEATED logo on the side panel. The sneaker also boasts an 8 and 24 printed on the inner heels as a nod to Bryant's jersey numbers and a classic gum sole.
