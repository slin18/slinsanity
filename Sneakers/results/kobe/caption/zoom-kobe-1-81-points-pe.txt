Zoom Kobe 1 '81 Points' PE | SKU: BAM241 M6 | Released: 2007-01-21


The Nike Zoom Kobe 1 '81 Points' PE honors five-time NBA champion Kobe Bryant and one of his most legendary performances. On January 22, 2006, Bryant dropped 81 points in a win over the Toronto Raptors, marking the second-highest point total in league history. This shoe is an unreleased Player Exclusive of the same shoe Kobe wore that game. Dressed in a Lakers-themed colorway of white, black and purple, the sneaker comes with a leather upper and Nike's Zoom Air technology.
