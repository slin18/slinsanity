Zoom Kobe 1 'Fade To Black' | SKU: 869451 110 | Released: 2016-03-23


This Nike Zoom Kobe 1 'FTB' features a Sail leather upper with snakeskin-like texture on the mudguard and side panel Swoosh. It also comes with debossed branding on the tongue and a tonal Sail sole. It dropped in March 2016 as part of Kobe’s ‘Fade to Black’ / ‘Black Mamba’ collection. The 13-part collection released special edition colorways for the entire Kobe signature shoe line, as well as the Hyperdunk and Huarache 2k4, leading up to Kobe’s last game in the NBA in April 2016.
