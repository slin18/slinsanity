The Nike Zoom Kobe 1 Protro ‘81 Points’ celebrates Kobe Bryant’s most prolific professional scoring performance, when he dropped 81 points against the Toronto Raptors in 2006. This Zoom Kobe 1 Protro recreates the shoe Kobe was wearing that night, updated with modern performance technologies that include a modified Zoom Air setup and a smaller carbon fiber shank.
