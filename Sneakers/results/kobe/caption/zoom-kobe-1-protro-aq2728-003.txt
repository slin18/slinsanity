Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'Del Sol' | SKU: AQ2728 003 | Released: 2018-02-17


Not all updates require aesthetic or design changes. Sometimes, improving the technology that goes into the shoe is precisely what that model needs to reboot itself. The Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'Del Sol' plays on the retro look of the 1997 Kobe 1 but adds a modern feel by creating the lightest version of the model yet with improved cushioning and a smaller carbon fiber shank. The sneaker boasts a black leather upper with a Varsity Maize knit collar, large white Swoosh branding, and a full-length Zoom midsole.
