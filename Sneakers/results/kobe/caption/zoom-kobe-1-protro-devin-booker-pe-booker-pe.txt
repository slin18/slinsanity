Zoom Kobe 1 Protro 'Devin Booker' PE | SKU: AR4598 500 | Released: 2018-04-11


NBA guard Devin Booker has been wearing Kobe Bryant silhouettes during his time with the Phoenix Suns. Receiving a special PE version of the Zoom Kobe 1 Protro, it features a Phoenix Suns’ inspired colorway with a purple patent leather upper and orange Swoosh. It also has an orange ‘1’ embroidered on the lateral heel and a ‘Suns’ tab on the tongue. The shoe was followed by the Kobe A.D. 'Devin Booker' PE later that month.
