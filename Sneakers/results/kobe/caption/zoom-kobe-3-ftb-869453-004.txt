Zoom Kobe 3 'Fade To Black | SKU: 869453 004 | Released: 2016-03-28


To celebrate Kobe Bryant’s storied career, Nike released the ‘Fade To Black’ Collection leading up to his last game in April 2016. The collection included the Huarache 2k4, the Nike Hyperdunk 2008 and Kobe’s 11 signature models, with tonal colorways fading from solid white on the Huarache 2k4 to black on the Kobe 11 Elite Low. This Nike Zoom Kobe 3 ‘FTB’ features a tonal Matte Silver colorway on the Kobe 3’s unique web-like design, which he laced up during the 2007-2008 NBA season.
