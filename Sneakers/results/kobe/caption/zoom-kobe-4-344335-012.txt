Zoom Kobe 4 'Bred' | SKU: 344335 012 | Released: 2009-05-01


Designed by Eric Avar and Tom Luedecke and launched in 2008, the Nike Zoom Kobe 4 was the first Kobe sneaker to be released in low-top form. This 2009 Zoom Kobe 4 ‘Bred’ features a classic colorway, with a black upper complemented by hits of white and Varsity Red, including on the outsole. Supported underfoot with Zoom Air cushioning, the heel counter sports Kobe Bryant’s signature.
