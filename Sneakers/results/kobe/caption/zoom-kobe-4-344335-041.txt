Zoom Kobe 4 'MPLS' | SKU: 344335 041 | Released: 2009-03-21


Designed by Eric Avar and Tom Luedecke, the Kobe 4 features Flywire on the upper, Zoom Air cushioning, and a modified herringbone traction pattern. This Zoom Kobe 4 ‘MPLS' honors the Minneapolis Lakers, who launched the Lakers’ franchise in 1947 wearing jerseys emblazoned with ‘MPLS’ across the front. It features a Stealth Grey upper with University Blue accents, a white midsole, and a translucent University Blue outsole.
