Zoom Kobe 4 'Rice Home' | SKU: 344335 371 | Released: 2009-09-05


The Nike Zoom Kobe 4 ‘Rice’ was created for Rice High School in Harlem, New York. Based on the school’s home jersey, the shoe features a Deep Forest upper accented by Varsity Maize on the Swoosh, laces and lining. Varsity Maize Flywire cables provide added color and support, while a perforated white toe box matches the white midsole. Cushioned by Zoom Air and Lunarlon foam, a green outsole emerges underfoot.
