Zoom Kobe 4 POP 'Playoffs' | SKU: 354187 101 | Released: 2009-05-01


Designed by Eric Avar and Tom Luedecke, the Nike Zoom Kobe 4 was the first Kobe sneaker released in low-top form. The Nike Zoom Kobe 4 POP ‘Del Sol’ dropped in a 2009 Playoff Pack. Featuring a predominantly black and white upper, Del Sol accents emerge on the collar, Swoosh and laces. A custom ‘Playoff Pack’ logo on the tongue features Kobe Bryant’s ‘Mamba’ nickname. The fit is supported by Flywire cables on the upper, a Lunar Foam midsole and Zoom Air cushioning.
