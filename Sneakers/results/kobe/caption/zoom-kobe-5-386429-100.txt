Zoom Kobe 5 'Draft Day' | SKU: 386429 100 | Released: 2010-06-01


The Zoom Kobe 5 ‘Draft Day’ features a white-based upper, tonal Flywire cables, and speckled laces. The sneaker also sports a light blue mesh heel for added motion flexibility and breathability, Varsity Purple details on the tongue, collar lining, and inner lining, a synthetic heel counter for structure, and leather details on the branded tongue. This colorway is an homage to the Charlotte Hornets, who drafted Kobe and traded him to the Lakers.
