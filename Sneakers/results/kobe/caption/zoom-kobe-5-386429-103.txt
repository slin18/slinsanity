Zoom Kobe 5 'USA' | SKU: 386429 103 | Released: 2010-08-06


The Nike Zoom Kobe 5 ‘USA’ delivers a patriotic take on Kobe Bryant’s fifth signature silhouette, highlighted by a white synthetic upper with contrasting Obsidian and Sport Red accents. The extremely lightweight build is strengthened through the application of heat-bonded overlays and strategically placed Flywire cables. A Phylon-injected midsole gives way to a grippy rubber outsole, fortified with a carbon fiber weave shank plate for midfoot rigidity.
