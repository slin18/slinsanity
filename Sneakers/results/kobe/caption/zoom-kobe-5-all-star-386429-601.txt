Zoom Kobe 5 'All Star' | SKU: 386429 601 | Released: 2010-02-06


The Nike Zoom Kobe 5 ‘All-Star’ was created for Kobe Bryant for the 2010 All-Star Game. The low-cut upper features a Daring Red Flywire upper with perforations on the toe box and a black external heel counter. The upper is mounted on a full-length black Phylon midsole alongside Nike Zoom units in heel and forefoot, supported by a midfoot shank plate and a translucent rubber outsole.
