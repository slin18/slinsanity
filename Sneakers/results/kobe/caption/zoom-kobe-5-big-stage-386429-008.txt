Zoom Kobe 5 'Big Stage Away' | SKU: 386429 008 | Released: 2010-08-06


The Nike Zoom Kobe 5 ‘Big Stage Away’ was worn by Kobe Bryant in the 2010 NBA Finals. The shoe features a black upper with gold accents and an all-over tattoo-style graphic depicting the highlights of Kobe’s season. Stats highlighted on the upper include his 27 average points-per-game and his 25,207 total career points, achieved on February 1, 2010 to make him the Lakers’ all-time leading scorer.
