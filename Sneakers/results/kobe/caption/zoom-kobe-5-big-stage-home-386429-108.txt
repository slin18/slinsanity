Zoom Kobe 5 'Big Stage Home' | SKU: 386429 108 | Released: 2010-08-06


Kobe Bryant wore the Nike Zoom Kobe 5 ‘Big Stage Home’ during the 2010 NBA Finals en route to his fifth NBA championship ring. The shoe features a white upper with an allover tattoo-style graphic showcasing the high points of Kobe’s season alongside gold accents. Stats highlighted on the upper include his 25,207 total career points, which he achieved on February 1, 2010 to help him become the Lakers’ all-time leading scorer.
