Zoom Kobe 4 'Carpe Diem' | SKU: 344335 001 | Released: 2009-01-17


This Nike Zoom Kobe 4 ‘Carpe Diem’ released in January 2009. Dressed in a combination of black, white, Varsity Purple and Del Sol, the low-cut shoe comes equipped with Flywire technology on the upper, while a Lunar Foam midsole and Zoom Air cushioning provide support underfoot. The design also includes Kobe Bryant’s signature on the heel counter, an icy translucent outsole and an Italian sports car-style 'Carpe Diem’ logo on the tongue.
