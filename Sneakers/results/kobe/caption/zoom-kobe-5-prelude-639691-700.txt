Zoom Kobe 5 'Prelude' | SKU: 639691 700 | Released: 2014-01-04


Released in Nike’s Prelude Pack in January 2014, the Nike Zoom Kobe 5 'Prelude' celebrates Kobe Bryant’s impact on popular culture with a pop art-inspired colorway. The multicolor upper features Flywire technology for a locked-down fit, while the gold Swoosh serves as a tribute to the championship ring Bryant won while wearing the Kobe 5. It also comes with purple speckling on the black midsole and a glow-in-the-dark outsole underfoot.
