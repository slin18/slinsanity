Zoom Kobe 6 'Purple Gradient' | SKU: 429659 010 | Released: 2011-05-05


The Nike Zoom Kobe 6 ‘Purple Gradient’ was laced up by Kobe Bryant during the 2011 NBA season. It features a black and Concord gradient on the polyurethane upper, with a snakeskin-textured upper accented by metallic silver on the tongue and side wall Swoosh. It also comes with a black tumbled leather heel panel, a white external heel counter and Zoom Air cushioning in the heel and forefoot.
