Zoom Kobe 6 'Lakers Home' | SKU: 429659 104 | Released: 2011-10-01


Nike gave the Zoom Kobe 6 a Lakers-inspired look in August 2011. Nicknamed the ‘Lakers Home’ edition, the purple and yellow design is distinguished by a white synthetic upper with silver snake scale texturing throughout. Other signature details include two-tone laces, purple Zoom-cushioning embedded in the midsoles, and yellow Kobe Sheath logos, Swooshes, and speckling.
