Zoom Kobe 6 'All Star - Orange County' | SKU: 448693 800 | Released: 2011-02-17


Ahead of the 2011 NBA All-Star Game, Nike released a special Zoom Kobe 6 pack comprising four colorways paying homage to the Southland. This ‘Orange County’ edition features an all-over graphic using brilliant orange, yellow and red hues to depict a Southern California sunset. An illustrated starfish on the sockliner and contrasting pops of teal on the Swoosh, heel and outsole add to the coastal design theme.
