Zoom Kobe 6 'China' | SKU: 429659 006 | Released: 2011-03-05


To celebrate Kobe Bryant’s following in China, Nike created the ‘China’ rendition of the Kobe 6. The shoe incorporates a snake inspired design on the upper, featuring a metallic pewter snakeskin embossing that’s contrasted with an Ink Swoosh and Crimson Kobe branding. Nike Zoom is included for cushioning, while ‘VENOMENON’ is stamped onto the forefoot section of the midsole.
