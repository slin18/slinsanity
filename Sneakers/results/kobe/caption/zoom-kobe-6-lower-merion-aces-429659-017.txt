Zoom Kobe 6 'Lower Merion Aces' | SKU: 429659 017 | Released: 2011-09-10


This Zoom Kobe 6 ‘Lower Merion Aces’ is a tribute Kobe Bryant’s alma mater, Philadelphia’s Lower Merion High School. Decked out in the school’s colors, the shoe combines Cool Grey and metallic platinum hues on its snakeskin textured upper alongside a Varsity Crimson Swoosh. The design also features a grey midsole with Zoom Air cushioning, a Pure Platinum outsole and the basketball team’s logo, the Aces, on the lateral heel.
