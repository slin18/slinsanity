Zoom Kobe 6 Supreme 'Rice' | SKU: 446442 301 | Released: 2011-03-05


The Zoom Kobe 6 Supreme ‘Rice’ honors Rice High School, in Harlem, as a part of a group of schools that Kobe sponsors. The Zoom Kobe 6 Supreme 'Rice' features a black and metallic moss-green Polyurethane upper with black nubuck accents on the tongue and heel. The sneaker also boasts Green Apple details on the Swoosh and translucent outsole. A black mamba snakehead is embossed at the heel and Rice High’s logo appears on the medial side.
