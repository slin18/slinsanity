Zoom Kobe 6 'Vault Anniversary' | SKU: 446897 992 | Released: 2011-09-17


The Nike Kobe 6 ‘Vault Anniversary’ celebrated the first anniversary of Los Angeles’ Nike Vault located outside Staples Center. The black textured upper features a gold Swoosh, while further gold emerges on the speckling of the midsole. Grey accents appear on the liner and tongue, with an icy blue translucent outsole underfoot. Limited to just 24 pairs, the Nike Vault exclusive release comes packaged with a matching AW-77 hoodie in a special Nike Vault safety deposit box.
