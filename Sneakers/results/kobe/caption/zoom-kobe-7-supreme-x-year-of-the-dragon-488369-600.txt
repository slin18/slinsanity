Zoom Kobe 7 Supreme X 'Year Of The Dragon' | SKU: 488369 600 | Released: 2012-03-17


The limited edition Nike Zoom Kobe 7 Supreme X ‘Year of the Dragon’ dropped in March 2012 to celebrate the Year of the Dragon. The Action Red and Team Red upper is complemented by Lush Teal on the heel counter and inner bootie, while Electrolime branding, including the Swoosh and debossed dragon on the heel, contrasts the design. The System Supreme edition of the Kobe 7 comes with two interchangeable insert options.
