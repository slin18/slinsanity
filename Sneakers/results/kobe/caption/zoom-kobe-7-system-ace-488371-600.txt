Zoom Kobe 7 System 'Lower Merion Aces' | SKU: 488371 600 | Released: 2012-03-17


Released during the 2011-2012 NBA season, the Nike Kobe Zoom 7 System offers interchangeable cushioning systems—‘Attack Fast' with Zoom Air and ‘Attack Strong’ with full-length Cushlon. This limited edition Nike Zoom Kobe 7 System 'Lower Merion Aces’ honors Kobe Bryant’s Philadelphia high school. The low-top shoe features a blend of Team Red, white and metallic silver hues inspired by the school’s colors.
