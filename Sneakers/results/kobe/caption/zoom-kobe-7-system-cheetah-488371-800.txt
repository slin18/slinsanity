Zoom Kobe 7 System 'Cheetah' | SKU: 488371 800 | Released: 2012-08-25


Paying tribute to Kobe Bryant’s killer instincts, the Nike Zoom Kobe 7 System ‘Cheetah’ features a Cheetah print on its black cast polyurethane upper and Medium Grey accents throughout. The shoe also comes equipped with Flywire technology and a 3-D heel clip for lock-down support. Underfoot, the low top features a Phylon midsole with Nike Zoom units in the forefoot and a glass reinforced composite shank in the midsole.
