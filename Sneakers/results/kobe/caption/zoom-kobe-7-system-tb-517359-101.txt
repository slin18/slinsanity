Zoom Kobe 7 System TB 'White Metallic Silver' | SKU: 517359 101 | Released: 2012-09-18


The Zoom Kobe 7 System TB is a member of Nike's Team Bank series that gives Kobe Bryant's seventh signature sneaker a two-tone colorway of white and Metallic Silver. First introduced in late 2011, the Kobe 7 System and its 'Attack Fast' midsole was created specifically for players who prefer a lighter, quicker style. Its premium upper features a predator-themed design, while Nike's Flywire technology and Zoom Air in the forefoot and heel promote support and cushioning.
