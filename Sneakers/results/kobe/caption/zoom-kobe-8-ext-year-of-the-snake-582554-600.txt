Zoom Kobe 8 EXT 'Year of the Snake - Red' | SKU: 582554 600 | Released: 2013-03-09


The Year of the Snake aligns with Mamba Mentality, and this special offering of the Zoom Kobe 8 commemorates Chinese Lunar New Year. The shoe replaces the usual mesh application on the upper for a tonal red suede with tan accenting throughout. The VacTech upper creates a molded fit that wraps the forefoot, while mesh accents emerge on the toe and quarter-panel to keep feet cool and comfortable.
