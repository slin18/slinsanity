Zoom Kobe Venomenon 4 'White University Gold' | SKU: 635578 107 | Released: 2014-08-05


The Nike Zoom Kobe Venomenon 4 'White University Gold' is a low top basketball sneaker that comes dressed in the colors of Kobe Bryant's team for all 20 of his NBA seasons. This shoe, released in August 2014, is nicknamed 'Lakers Home' with its colorway of white, black, University Gold and Wolf Grey. It features Kobe's Nike branding on the tongue and heel and a mostly-white upper with Flywire threading to promote a lightweight, supportive fit. The white, Nike Zoom-equipped midsole rides above a gold outsole.
